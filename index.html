Franck Hueso, better known by his stage name Carpenter Brut, is a French darksynth artist from Poitiers, France. He has released three EPs, EP I (2012), EP II (2013) and EP III (2015) which were collected and released together as the album Trilogy (2015). He later released three additional studio albums, Leather Teeth (2018), Blood Machines OST (2020), and Leather Terror (2022) along with one live album, CarpenterBrutLive (2017). He has also contributed original music to a variety of soundtracks, both for film and video games.

Style of synth

Carpenter Brut claims his relative anonymity is a deliberate artistic choice in order to place more importance on the music itself, rather than the identity of the musician behind it. He started writing music as Carpenter Brut with the intention of mixing sounds from horror films, metal, rock, and electronic music. In live performances Carpenter Brut is joined on stage by guitarist Adrien Grousset and drummer Florent Marcadet, both from the French metal band Hacride.

According to Frank Weso, the creator of the album, after the release of Trilogy, his previous creation (which is a compilation of his three mini-albums: EP I, EP II, EP III), there was almost no time to create an album, since since 2015 he was on regular tours. Carpenter Brut wanted to make Leather Teeth's music softer than his previous album, so he tried to make it much closer to the synthwave genre. Many people participated in the creation of the album, especially singers. The album was released at midnight on February 22/23 without any announcement. According to Frank Weso, the album's creator, Leather Teeth is the soundtrack to an alleged 1987 movie called The Cheerleader Effect (similar to "Cheerleader Effect"). The album also continues the plot shown in Trilogy. The music is a mixture of electronic music, metal and glam rock, which makes the sound of the album similar to the work of Perturbator, Dan Terminus and GosT.
